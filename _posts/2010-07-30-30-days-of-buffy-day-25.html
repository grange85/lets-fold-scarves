Day 25: Favourite Buffyverse Saying


Buffy is stuffed with sayings. It is a quote monster.

The first one I thought of is "note to self: religion freaky" which I certainly agree with as a sentiment.

Then there is "Oh, poor Watcher. Did your life pass before your eyes? Cup of tea, cup of tea, almost got shagged, cup of tea?" and from the same episode "That'll put marzipan in your pie plate, Bingo!" and ""You know, if we want her to be exactly, she'll never be exactly, the only really real Buffy is really Buffy, and she's gone" who?".

Talking of Buffybot, here's a puzzled Buffy: "The who-whatting-how-with-huh?"

More Buffy: "I think I speak for everyone here when I say, huh?"

A bit of Cordelia wisdom: "Tact is just not saying true stuff."

Naturally there is "I'm so evil, and skanky... and I think I'm kinda gay." and following from that: "can we just skip it? can you... can you just be kissing me now?"

Some Joan: "I think I know why Joan's the boss. I'm like a superhero or something."

This is why I love Buffy: all these riches and I still haven't decided.

Try "I didn’t jump. I took a tiny step, and there conclusions were." or "It's entirely pointy!" or "Oh my God. I’m cured! I want the boys!" or "Anchovies, anchovies, you’re so delicious. I love you more than all the other fishes."

Do I have to chose? OK, it's twenty to one and I promised myself an early night so on the 30th July at 00:41, my favourite Buffyverse saying is "The girl’s not playing with a full deck, Giles. She has almost no deck. She has a three."
