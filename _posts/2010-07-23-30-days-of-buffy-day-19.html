Day 19: Character You Like That Everyone Else Hates

I'm not keen on this question because I don't know who this mysterious everyone is. However, I'll make a stab and suggest that we are talking about characters like Riley, Dawn, and Kennedy.

I'm sticking with that trio and out of them I'll say that I like Dawn. I thought Michelle Trachtenberg did a pretty good job despite having to struggle with situations written too young for her and with a role that trod water in S6 and 7. How great would it have been to take her origin and develop a storyline for her using that. Or how about Dawn becoming a watcher? Or how about Dawn being something more interesting than a klepto?

I liked her introduction to the show and thought the Key was a great idea which led to one of the best season finales.
