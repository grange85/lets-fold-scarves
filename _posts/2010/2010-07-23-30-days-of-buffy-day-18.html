Day 18: Character Who Didn’t Get Enough Screen Time

This question is tantalising. If only it was asking another but closely related question! I can't think of many characters who didn't get enough screen time but I can think of plenty who didn't get enough decent dialogue or plotlines. I can also think of one or two who got too much screen time...

However, these one episode only characters came to mind quite easily: Marcie and Sunday. Marcie Ross was played by Clea DuVall and for that reason alone I would have liked to have seen her again. Sunday was a fun villain who may well have become tiresome but I would have liked to have seen that happen rather tha her single appearance.
