Day 21: Best Willow-centric Episode

This I will find simultaneously both harder and easier than Day 20's Spike question. Easier because it's straightforward for me to conjure up Willow episodes I particularly like and harder because I have to chose one.

It's not that hard actually but I'll get to that.

It could be "I, Robot...You, Jane" because she was funny when she was angry and except for the laughable computery stuff it wasn't that bad an episode.

It could be one of the episodes where her partner is as important to the story as she is such as 'Wild at Heart", "New Moon Rising", and "Tough Love". It could be a pissed off Willow episode like "Something Blue" except for me it's Buffy and Spike that make that episode outstanding.

As you know Crack Willow doesn't do anything for me so I can't even consider any episodes from S6 but I do like "Same Time, Same Place" from S7.

It's obvious what's missing and it's one of my favourite episodes of the entire show and it's the one where Alyson Hannigan is awesome: "Doppelgangland".
